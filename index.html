Welcome to Blossom Café

Blossom Café is a trendy vegan-friendly café and all-day brunch restaurant, situated in the middle of Paris. A feel-good formula with a homely appearance, right at the heart of the French capital. We believe in a sustainable future where plant-based food plays a more prominent role, which is why our menu is basically vegan. With add-ons we also cater to guests who do not wish to eat completely vegan.

Enjoy our famous all-day brunch with family, friends or even by yourself. ♥

All day tasty food, fresh drinks & cosy vibes !
